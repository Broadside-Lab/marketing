Who We Are

Small team. Big explosions.

Atomic Canoe is an independent game studio focused on turn-based strategy and tactical games. We believe the best games are the ones where every decision matters and every match tells a different story.

Strategy First

We design games around meaningful choices, not reflexes. If you can outsmart your opponent, you deserve to win.

Personality Built In

From AI opponents with distinct playstyles to hand-crafted scenarios, our games have character that keeps you coming back.

Respect Your Time

No filler, no grind. Our games are designed around satisfying sessions that fit into a real life.

Craft Over Scale

We'd rather ship one polished game than ten forgettable ones. Every mechanic earns its place.
